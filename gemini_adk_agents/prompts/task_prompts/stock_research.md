# Stock Research Task Prompt

## Task
Research the following stock/company: {stock_symbol}

## Required Information

1. **Company Overview**
   - Business description
   - Industry and sector
   - Key products/services
   - Market position

2. **Financial Health**
   - Recent revenue and earnings
   - Profit margins
   - Debt levels
   - Cash flow status

3. **Recent News**
   - Latest developments
   - Management changes
   - Product announcements
   - Regulatory news

4. **Technical Analysis**
   - Current price trend
   - Support/resistance levels
   - Volume patterns
   - Moving averages

5. **Investment Thesis**
   - Bull case (reasons to buy)
   - Bear case (reasons to avoid)
   - Key risks
   - Catalysts to watch

## Output Format

Provide a comprehensive research report with:
- Confidence level (High/Medium/Low)
- Data sources used
- Analysis date
- Recommendation summary
